Community Powered Automated Robotics

ShereCar is building an online community for people interested in automated robotics. Meet with fellow roboticists online and in person, and discuss projects through specifically built showcase platforms.

Self Driving Packages

We want to develop community contributed software and hardware packages that people can use to power their automated robots. Ultimately, we wish to provide a kit for self driving cars.

Online Discussion

Discuss automation and vision topics with the online community at the ShereCar forums. We've got a wide variety of topics available, such as engineering, artificial intelligence, and virtual worlds. You should feel free to introduce ones too.

Face to Face

Engage peers through a meetup group based on central Jersey focused on developing technologies for automated robotics such as self driving cars.

Upcoming and past events can be viewed at meetup.com, along with details and comments people have made about them.

Vision Exploratorium

This is the ShereCar Projects computer vision exploratorium! Here you will find computer vision references and tutorials in the form of challenges to help you get a better understanding of computer vision and how it is applies to autonomous vehicles.
